Interesting facts:

  • Jumping spiders have 8 legs and have 8 eyes!
  • They have colored vision and their vision is comparable to that of the humans.
  • Most of them can jump 50 times their own body length, giving them the much needed element of surprise while hunting.
  • They are capable of agile jumps. They have an internal hydraulic system that alters the body fluid pressure
    and extends their legs, allowing them to jump high.
  • Jumping spiders can climb on glass.
